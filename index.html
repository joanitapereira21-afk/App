HOW TO PLAY

This is a symbol decoder game and it's up to you to help the analyst recover the code

  1. Tap a symbol in the grid.
  2. The hidden number appears for 1 second.
  3. When the time is up, the symbol comes back. The number disappears.

You will need this to form the correct formation of numbers to proceed to the next minigame.

ABOUT THIS GAME

Flight 4.0.4 Decoder is part of an immersive cybersecurity training project created with small and medium-sized businesses in mind.

Instead of long presentations, this game lets employees practise making decisions in a safe, playful way. By linking strange symbols to numbers, players get used to slowing down, paying attention, and thinking before they click.

The game sits alongside other training tools to help people understand how everyday actions can protect data, reduce risks like phishing and scams, and build a safer digital workplace.
